The number of confirmed COVID-19 cases per capita for each US county is higher for counties with a larger population size than for those with a smaller population size.

COVID-19 cases per Capita of Counties in the United States as of October 1, 2020

Covid-19 cases per capita is determined by calculating the percentage of confirmed cases for a county (as of October 1, 2020) to the total number of residents in the county.
